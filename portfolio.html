My Projects

Bounty Hunter

Bounty Hunter is an AI-powered public safety system that helps law enforcement by detecting and identifying wanted criminals in public spaces. It uses facial recognition and computer vision to scan real-time video feeds from CCTV cameras or smart glasses and matches detected faces against a criminal database.

Study Mate

Study Mate is an AI tool that helps students learn from their PDFs like textbooks and notes. Students can upload PDFs, ask questions in natural language, and get clear answers. It uses AI and semantic search to find the most relevant content from the document. This makes studying faster, easier, and more effective.

Zenith Brain Institution Website

The Zenith Brain Institution Website is a digital education management platform built with Python (Django), MySQL, and a responsive frontend. It streamlines operations by offering modern interfaces for students, parents, tutors, and administrators. The system includes features such as course management, attendance monitoring, progress tracking, and communication tools. By centralizing academic and administrative tasks, Zenith improves efficiency and enhances the overall learning experience.
